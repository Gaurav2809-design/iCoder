How to Write for Us at iCoder

Follow these simple steps to submit your article and join our community of contributors!

Content Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines to ensure your article is accepted:

  • Original Content: We only accept original articles that haven’t been published elsewhere.
  • Length: Articles should be between 800 and 1,500 words. Longer pieces are acceptable for in-depth tutorials or industry analyses.
  • Structure: Use clear headings (H2, H3) to structure your article. Make sure it’s easy to scan and read.
  • Clarity: Write in a clear, conversational tone. Avoid overly technical jargon unless it’s necessary for your audience.
  • SEO Best Practices: Naturally integrate relevant keywords into your article. Don’t keyword-stuff, but make your content valuable to readers.
  • Code Examples: Include relevant code examples with proper formatting. Use a code block for better readability.
  • Visuals: Include screenshots, diagrams, or any visuals that enhance your article. Please make sure you have the rights to use them and give appropriate credit if necessary.
  • Links: You can include up to [x] external links, but ensure they are relevant and add value.

Article Types We Accept

We accept a wide range of articles related to coding, technology, and software development. Here are some examples:

  • Tutorials and How-To Guides: Step-by-step instructions on coding languages, frameworks, or tools.
  • Industry News: Keep our readers up-to-date with the latest trends and updates in tech.
  • Product Reviews: Reviews of new coding tools, tech gadgets, or software platforms.
  • Opinion Pieces: Share your thoughts on coding trends or tech industry predictions.
  • Personal Stories: Write about your experience as a developer and lessons learned.

How to Submit Your Article

Here’s the process for submitting your article:

  1. Step 1: Submit a Pitch: Email us a brief outline of your article idea.
  2. Step 2: Write Your Article: Once your pitch is approved, write the full article following our content guidelines.
  3. Step 3: Submit the Full Article: Send your article in Word or Google Docs format for review.
  4. Step 4: Article Review: Our editorial team will review your submission and may request revisions.
  5. Step 5: Publication: Once approved, your article will be published on iCoder.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us at iCoder01@gmail.com.
